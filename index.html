What Is Modular Construction

A Smarter Way
to Build Beautifully

Modular homes are precision-built in sections inside a climate-controlled factory, then transported and assembled on your permanent foundation — meeting or exceeding every local and state building code. The result: superior craftsmanship, less weather exposure, less waste, and a faster path to the home you've always imagined.

01
Factory-Built Precision
Every module is constructed indoors by skilled craftspeople, eliminating weather delays and ensuring consistent quality control unavailable on a traditional job site.
02
Code-Compliant by Design
Modular homes meet the same local, state, and regional building codes as site-built homes — and are often inspected multiple times throughout construction.
03
Permanent Foundation
Assembled on a traditional permanent foundation on your land, a Biviano home appreciates in value just like any custom site-built residence.
